Initial research and concepts

For my game my initial idea was to have a game set in the doctor who universe. The game would revolve around the weeping angles, these are stone statues that move when not observed.

My initial research for the 3d models revolved around the sets they used for the tv show. The sets they used were a run-down house, a city park, and a cave. Out of these sets I decided to take the city park style approach more as it seemed more fitting.

Using the statue like appearance I decided to research as to where statues would be without them looking out of place, I then decided to have the game take place in a maze as most mazes tend to have a stone statue of sorts, so it seemed very fitting.

My current game idea takes inspiration from the Stephen king film in the tall grass where the tall grass field becomes an ever-changing maze. I have deviated from this filed area by having the age take place inside Henver castles yew maze, this is to allow the game to have a more realistic approach also having the game take place near a castle allows me to harken back to medieval folklore as to better explain the enemy.

The enemy has seen a change in the initial idea as originally it was supposed to be a stone angle or a figure but after much deliberation, I concluded that a stone figure wouldn’t fit in as well and would seem like the clear antagonist or obstacle of the game. So, I chose to have the enemy be able to blend in with the environment. I decided to have the enemy be a tree folk which is a tree like humanoid, they have appeared in modern media with the trading card game magic the gathering and the lord of the ring’s series.
